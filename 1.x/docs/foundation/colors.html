Colors

The colors used on the FCC site are carefully selected to be both attractive and user friendly. Each color has been tested to meet 508 compliance standards.

The colors below are labeled with their hex code, whether or not white type can be used on the color, and whether or not the color can be used in type that is on a white background. Situations where type meets 508 contrast are labeled with “Type OK” and a checkmark. Situations where the type does not meet 508 contrast are labeled with a dash.
